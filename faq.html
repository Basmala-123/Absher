Becoming a remote telemarketer

Is there a fee to join be-paid-daily.online?

No, there are no fees involved to join our network of customer care pros. We will never ask you for banking/financial information during the application process.

Do I need to apply more than once if there are multiple accounts that interest me?

No, you only need to apply to join the be-paid-daily.online once. Once you are part of our network of remote telemarketer pros, you will be able to view all open and active campaigns and select the client program you feel suits you best based on your experience, availability, and interest. We do not allow Freelancers to have more than one candidate profile.

Where can I work?

All of our telemarketer pros work from home, as long as they are located. We accept a worldwide candidature.

I’ve passed the assessment. What comes next?

Candidates who qualify will receive an email invitation to login to our Skype group to complete a technical installation of our dialer. Once you complete the installation, you will be able to view all open and active campaigns and can select the client campaign that interests you the most.

How do I know if I passed the assessment?

We will notify you by email within 24 hours of completing the assessment. If you are among the qualified candidates, you will also receive your invitation to join our Skype group where you will complete the final steps of our application process

Can I retake the assessment?

Given the high-volume of applications that we receive, we do not allow candidates to retake the assessment. Please make sure you take the assessment in a distraction-free space and have plenty of time to complete it.

How do I select a client campaign?

Once you complete the installation of our dialer, you will be able to view all open and active campaigns. Select the account that interests you the most and fill in the form. We will notify you by email if you were selected for the client campaign. Please make sure to complete all of the required fields to avoid any delays.

How do I prepare for my account?

Once you are selected to work on a client campaign, you will go through virtual training to learn the script and rebuttals before starting to work. Instruction can take anywhere from two days to several weeks, depending on the campaign. There is no cost or fees for training.

Work and compensation

What will my schedule be?

Our telemarketer pros are able to schedule their work hours. When you join the be-paid-daily.onine, you work when it’s convenient for you.

Are the opportunities part time or full time?

Our working hours range between 15 to 40+ hours per week. Minimum requested hours vary depending on client campaign.

Can I work on more than one client campaign at a time?

Yes. Once you have successfully completed at least 50 hours on one of our campaigns, you will be eligible to add more client campaigns to your workload.

How much can I earn?

The pay structure varies depending on the client campaign. Some of our accounts are paid for having made an appointment setting, while others are paid for a task of buffer minutes, sales, or a combination of both. Please refer to the job description of the opportunity that you are interested in for more information.

How will I get paid?

ACC pays via three methods:

  • 1- Paypal
  • 2- Western Union mandate
  • 3- Wire bank transfer

Do you provide benefits?

Since our remote telemarketers are independent freelancers, we cannot offer traditional benefits such as paid time off and health insurance. However, there are many perks to joining the be-paid-daily.online:

  • Work from home with daily payment
  • No commuting hassles
  • Schedule your hours
  • Work when it’s convenient for you
  • Gain experience in different industries
  • High pay rate

Yes absolutely it is free … but from the second payment we’ll deduce the amount of 20€ per month for your Voip minutes consummation. This act will be repeated monthly.
